Discover Heat Vulnerability Mapping Tool

Heat vulnerability varies spatially across a region, disproportionately affecting vulnerable populations including older adults, pregnant women, outdoor workers and the unhoused. The Heat vulnerability mapping tool was created to engage communities in equitable heat resilience measures.
